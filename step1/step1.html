Step-by-step guide on using 3Mensio to find Annulo-papillary distance

3Mensio Set Up
The very basic step of using 3Mensio is importing the patient data. Data can be in both forms, either MRI or CT scans.

Import the patient data by clicking on import button on the left , click browse.

Open the file of the patient who is undergoing the Mitral Valve Replacement Surgery. You can also have all the files saved in local archive.You will see all the patient data you have imported. Double click on the patient you want to select.

Choose “Mitral valve” when asked to choose heart structure.
Roughly locate the mitral valve and click on “mitral assessment”.
You will see four different segment views of the heart.
